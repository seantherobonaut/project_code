About Us:

Our company streches way back to the 1930's on a family farm that always handed out cookies on lunch break. As time went on and as word spread, other farmers came to get these amazing cookies too that helped make their days just a little better.

Eventually, demand became too much for a handful of people. This is where the first beginnings of ABC cookies came into existance. It started by using one entire kitchen to keep up with the demand but eventually required several.

Eventually, the company moved into a factory and started a larger scale manufacturing process that started shipping all over the world.

Just recently the company decided to upload a website to make their goods available online instead of just in catalogs. This really got the business to take off into what it is today.
