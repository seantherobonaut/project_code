Students on FIRST robotic teams are mentored through the robot design and build process by dedicated volunteer mentors. Currently our team of mentors includes an aerospace engineer, an electronics engineer, two software engineers, a project development engineer, a mechanical engineer, an electronics engineering college student, and an assortment of generous, skilled parents and community members. We need your help to raise money to fund our team and keep us competitive with the hundreds of teams in the competitions.


In order to help out our team you can:
  • comment on our blog
  • mentor us
  • mail us a check
  • inspire others to follow us
  • donate to us online!
