Here are some words about them!
Our Mentors are all volunteer professionals, parents, and community members with a passion for inspiring our community’s youth in the fields of science and technology. All of our mentors are amazing, talented people who generously share their knowledge and experience with students.

Our build mentors set a great example as they keep coming back and persevere through this project with steadfast patience. Mr.Reif, Mr. van Dyk, Mr. Bergman are our engineering mentors. Mr. Reif, is working as an Aerospace Engineer for Pumpkin, Inc. Mr. van Dyk is a Development Engineer at SRI International and has experience mentoring the Gunn High School Robotics Team in Palo Alto. Mr. Bergman is currently a Business and Systems Consultant. Mr. Lindborg and Tmag (Tim Magoffin) are also important Build mentors. Mr. Lindborg is a Prosthetist and Clinical Business Development Manager for Touch Bionics, Inc. Tmag is our college student, Circuit Breaker alumni mentor – specialty, Electronics. On the computer programming side, we are fortunate to have Mr. Rutten and Mr. Donlon. Mr. Rutten is Development Technical Lead at X.commerce, a division of eBay. Mr. Donlon is a Software Engineer at Intel Corp.

None of this would be possible without another group of very important mentors. Debby Hanson, is our school advisor and supports us with logistics, communications and everything else we can think of! Ms. Lindborg has a BA in Management, (thank goodness) and is our Team Coordinator. She is our glue. Sherri Donlon is a Professional Website Designer assisting our team webmaster with website design and maintenance. Ady Langer is our Fueling Mentor. She feeds us during the Build Season while we are putting in long hours building the robot.

In addition to our Mentors, we have many, parents, school district employees, and community members who support us by sending materials, helping with IT, office and custodial needs, and stopping in and helping with the Build when they can. THANK YOU TO ALL OF YOU!! Each of you is an important part of our team.
