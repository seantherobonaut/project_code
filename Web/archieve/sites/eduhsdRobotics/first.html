Dean Kamen's passion and determination to help young people discover the excitement and rewards of science and technology are the cornerstones of FIRST ("For Inspiration and Recognition of Science and Technology"). (http://www.usfirst.org)

FIRST is a rapidly growing organization that aims to capture the interest of all in order to create a better future. They do this by creating a fiercely competitive environment where creativity is welcome to thrive and problem solving is just another form of fun. This is an opportunity that gives participants the chance learn about science and technology at a very advanced level and still have a great time. Every year, teams compete like crazy against each other but are graciously professional about it. There are regional competitions and then the final competition which is made up of the winning teams of each regional competition.
