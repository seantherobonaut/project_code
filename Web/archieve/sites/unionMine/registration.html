How to Register at Union Mine

Use the following setps to guide yourself in registering a student to Union Mine High School. Please be aware that you must live in Union Mine District Area to register and attend Union Mine. If you have any questions, please call the Counseling Office for help/clarification @ 1-(530)-621-4003 x4210

  1. Registration Form
  2. Complete the Home Language Survey
  3. Complete the New Student Enrollment Information
  4. Fill out the Emergency Card Form
  5. Fill out the EDUHSD Computer User Agreement
Contact Sam Felcher (UMHS Registrar) for an appointment at 1-(530)-621-4003 x4210
