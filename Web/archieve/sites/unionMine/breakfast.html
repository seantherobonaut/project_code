Breakfast Menu

All students have a personal identification number (PIN) for their own breakfast/lunch account. Parents & students can add money to their account, allowing them to buy food from the snack bars & lunch line. Students can find out their PIN by asking one of the cafeteria personnel to look it up.

Remember all students (even reduced meal students) have a PIN.

Breakfast Meal Prices

  • Reduced Breakfast - $0.30
  • Adult Breakfast - $2.00
